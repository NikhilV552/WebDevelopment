The Time Table App

This a system used for preparing the time table during the beginning of the semester in engineering colleges or during the beginning of the acedemic year in the schools.
As in most of the engineering colleges students have to move to different classrooms to attend different classes, there might be a classroom alloted to two different classes at a time. This app is helpful in detecting those clashes which arise in the timetable so that these don't take place or even the case may be for the single teacher alloted to teach two classes at the same time.
As you may see the pics of this App you can check Day wise if there are any clashes and even with the whole time table i.e all the 6 days at once.
The security is not implemented to have the edit option only for admin .
The "Check Row" button is used to check the particular day only with the other class time table already present or prepared via this app and the "Check" button is to check the whole Time table.
